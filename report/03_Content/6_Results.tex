construction run and the arena evaluation configured in
Chapter~\ref{ch:experimental-design}, structured around the
research-question/diagnostic mapping of Table~\ref{tab:rq-map}: the

research question RQ1, and the migration-effect research question
RQ2. All headline numbers are to be reported as mean $\pm$ standard
deviation over the three seeds $\{42, 137, 2048\}$ with bootstrap
confidence intervals, per the statistical protocol of
Section~\ref{sec:exp-metrics}.
